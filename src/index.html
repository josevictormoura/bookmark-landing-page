A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quik easy for you to access your favorite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

  • Simple BookMarking
  • Speedy Shearching
  • Easy Sharing

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in on time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Bookmark in one click

Easily share your bookmarks and colletions with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
